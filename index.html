Color Story

Using a variety of shades of pink keeps are branding consistent. Each color has its own
unique message to our customers:

Baby Pink

This shade is for childrens toys and sleepwear.

Hot Pink

This shade is for high energy marketing.

Rose Pink

This shade is for PR releases and scandals.

Salmon Pink

This shade must be avoided. Barbie DOES NOT wear salmon pink.
